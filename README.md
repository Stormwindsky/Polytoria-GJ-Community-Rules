# 🏰 Polytoria Community Rules (Gamejolt)

Welcome to the official **Polytoria** community on Gamejolt! To keep this space fun, creative, and safe for all users, please follow the guidelines below.

---

## 1. Follow Gamejolt’s Terms of Service
First and foremost, all members must adhere to [Gamejolt’s Site Guidelines](https://gamejolt.com/guidelines). Harassment, hate speech, NSFW content, and illegal activities are strictly prohibited.

## 2. Keep it Polytoria-Related
This community is dedicated to **Polytoria**. All posts, fan art, and discussions should focus on Polytoria’s gameplay, economy, avatars, or development.
* **Off-topic posts** may be removed to keep the feed clean.

## 3. The "Roblox" Policy
To maintain our unique identity, **general Roblox content is strictly prohibited.**
* **Do not** post Roblox memes, gameplay, or advertisements.
* **Exceptions:** Discussion of Roblox is *only* permitted if it directly relates to Polytoria (e.g., comparing technical features, discussing cross-platform concepts, or mentioning "crossplay" ideas). 
* *Note: Using Roblox as a way to insult Polytoria or its users will result in an immediate post removal.*

## 4. No Spam or Self-Promotion
* Do not spam the feed with repetitive posts.
* Do not promote your own social media, Discord servers, or non-Polytoria projects without moderator permission.
* Advertising your Polytoria items, showcases, or places is encouraged!

## 5. Be Respectful
Treat your fellow "Polytorians" with respect. Constructive criticism of the platform is allowed, but toxic behavior toward staff or other users is not tolerated.

## 6. Trading and Economy Safety
* Be careful when discussing trades. 
* **Scamming is a permanent ban offense.** * Do not post "deals" that require users to share their passwords or personal information.

---

> **Failure to follow these rules may lead to a warning, post deletion, or a ban from the community depending on the severity of the infraction.**

*Thank you for being part of our community and helping Polytoria grow!*
